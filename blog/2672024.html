The world of personal computing is on the cusp of a revolution with the introduction of the XT-16, the first iteration of the groundbreaking PWED (Portable Workspace Enderrensing Device). This innovative device empowers users to break free from the confines of traditional operating systems and craft a truly personalized digital workspace. Power in Your Pocket Despite its compact size, the XT-16 packs a punch. Here's a glimpse into its core specifications:

  • Memory: 16KB RAM - While seemingly modest, this RAM capacity, coupled with nanoOS's efficient design, ensures smooth operation for essential tasks.
  • Storage: 16GB Flash Storage - Ample space to store your essential applications, files, and configurations.
  • Connectivity: USB 2.0 - Seamlessly connect the XT-16 to any computer, facilitating data transfer and powering the device.
  • Transfer Speed: 3MBytes/s - Efficient data transfer for managing your digital workspace.
  • Power: 5V - Powered conveniently through your computer's USB port, eliminating the need for additional power sources.
  • Operating System: nanoOS version 0.1.95-delta - This custom-built, TypeScript-powered OS forms the heart of the PWED experience, offering an open-source, highly customizable platform.
